## üé® ADVANCED MIDJOURNEY TIPS

### Style Parameters to Use:
- `--ar 16:9` ‚Üí Wide landscape (Facebook feed, website)
- `--ar 1:1` ‚Üí Square (Instagram feed)
- `--ar 4:5` ‚Üí Portrait (Instagram feed, stories)
- `--ar 9:16` ‚Üí Vertical (Instagram stories, reels)
- `--style raw` ‚Üí More photorealistic, less artistic
- `--v 6` ‚Üí Latest version (better quality)
- `--s 250` ‚Üí Stylization (lower = more realistic)

### Quality Enhancement:
Add these to any prompt for better results:
```
, professional stock photography, high resolution, Canon EOS R5, 50mm f/1.4 lens,
natural lighting, 8k quality, sharp focus, photorealistic
```

### Indian Skin Tone Specification:
```
, Indian features, brown skin tone (medium to dark), authentic Indian appearance
```

### Age Specification:
```
, late 20s, early 30s, mid 40s (be specific)
```
